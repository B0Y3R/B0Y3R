# James Boyer

**Engineering manager, Core Mobile at Ava Labs.** I build secure crypto wallets on iOS and Android, and lead the teams that ship them.

Most of my depth is in high-trust mobile: passkeys and FIDO2, wallet recovery, encryption, and crypto custody, from my time at Casa and Chamber. Before software I diagnosed cars for Mercedes-Benz and Nissan, which is still how I think. Systems, root cause, and craft under pressure.

### What I work on
- Secure mobile for crypto: native iOS and Android, React Native, Flutter
- Passkeys and FIDO2, wallet recovery, and encryption
- Leading distributed mobile teams

### What I'm building and researching
- **[travel-agent](https://github.com/B0Y3R/travel-agent):** a Claude Code plugin that researches a destination, helps you pick a place to stay, and builds a shareable guide. My playground for practical AI agents.
- **civilian:** a check-in based safety app. Arm it before a situation that might be sketchy, and if you miss a check-in it sends your last known location to the people you trust. Only escalates when you actually need help. (private for now)
- **Bitcoin and Liquid treasury custody:** wallet-agnostic UTXO and PSBT coordinators with real role-based access, built with family offices and BTC/Liquid treasuries in mind. Ongoing research.

### What I'm thinking about
Where crypto and AI meet, from a user-security point of view. Proof-of-human protocols. Zero-trust, zero-knowledge systems that assume nothing and reveal nothing. Backups that actually survive, and running things on prem so you own the whole stack. And the thing under all of it: keeping people anonymous while making the mundane stuff easy and approachable, so they can close the laptop and spend more time *(in meat space)* enjoying real life.
